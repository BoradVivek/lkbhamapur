Organizational Structure

Our school is meticulously organized to ensure efficient administration, effective teaching, and comprehensive student support. The structure is designed to foster collaboration among staff and provide clear lines of communication.

  • School Management Committee: Oversees the strategic direction and policy-making.
  • Principal: The head of the institution, responsible for academic and administrative leadership.
  • Vice Principal: Supports the Principal in day-to-day operations and academic supervision.
  • Heads of Departments (HODs): Lead and manage specific academic disciplines (e.g., Science, Mathematics, Languages, Commerce).
  • Teaching Faculty: Dedicated and experienced educators for Secondary and Higher Secondary levels.
  • Administrative Staff: Manages admissions, records, finance, and general school operations.
  • Counseling and Support Services: Provides academic guidance, career counseling, and emotional support to students.
  • Student Councils & Clubs: Empower student leadership and promote extracurricular engagement.

This structure ensures that all facets of school life are managed with precision, creating a harmonious and productive learning environment for everyone.
