Our Glorious Achievements

At SMT Labhuben Kanjibhai Babariya Secondary and Higher Secondary School Hamapur, we pride ourselves on a consistent record of excellence, both academically and in extracurricular activities. Our students and faculty continually strive for and achieve remarkable feats.

Consistently High Board Results

Achieving a pass percentage of over 95% in both Secondary and Higher Secondary Board Examinations for the last five years.

State-Level Sports Champions

Our athletic teams have secured multiple championships in various state-level competitions including Kho-Kho, Kabaddi, and Athletics.

National Science Exhibition Winners

Students' innovative projects have won top honors at the National Science & Technology Exhibitions, showcasing their scientific aptitude.

Cultural Excellence Awards

Awarded 'Best Cultural Institution' at regional festivals for our vibrant folk dance, drama, and music performances.

Active Community Engagement

Recognized for significant contributions to local community service initiatives, including environmental drives and literacy programs.

Distinguished Alumni Network

Our alumni have achieved success in diverse fields, with many pursuing higher education at prestigious national and international universities.
